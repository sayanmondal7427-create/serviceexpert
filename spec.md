# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Replace the Services page service catalog with a new list of 10 service categories and remove any garbled or unclear text from the page.

**Planned changes:**
- Remove all existing service items from the Services page.
- Add 10 new service categories with their sub-services, each labeled in both Bengali and English:
  - Fridge (Installation, Repair)
  - Washing Machine (Installation, Repair)
  - AC – Air Conditioner (Installation, Repair, Service/Cleaning)
  - Geyser (Installation, Repair)
  - Induction Cooktop (Repair)
  - Chimney (Installation, Repair, Cleaning)
  - Liner (Installation, Repair)
  - Wiring/Electrical (New Wiring, Repair)
  - Microwave Oven (Repair)
  - Aqua Guard Filter (Installation, Repair)
- Audit and clean up all headings, labels, descriptions, and button text on the Services page to remove any garbled, placeholder, or unclear text.

**User-visible outcome:** The Services page displays a clean, professional catalog of 10 service categories with bilingual (Bengali and English) labels and sub-services, with no confusing or garbled text anywhere on the page.
